Risk Analysis & Strategic Countermeasures

Identified Risks

  • Stressed Bandwidth & Burnout: Losing 3 resources shrinks our current limited bandwidth until new hire is up to speed.
  • Tight Deadline: There isn't much time to transfer all the needed knowledge before Alex leaves.
  • Risk to Daily Work: Important reports and vendor tasks could be missed after Alex's last day.
  • Hiring Delays: If we don't hire someone by July 14, the entire plan is at risk.
  • Training Overload: Melodie is new and needs a lot of training, which could be stressful.

Mitigation Plan

  • Fast-Track Hiring: Speed up the hiring process for the new person with HR's help.
  • Focused Knowledge Transfer: Hold daily training sessions with Alex to capture the most important info.
  • Share the Workload: Move some of Alex's tasks to other team members to free him up.
  • Have a Backup Plan: Line up extra help from HRIT and our vendors just in case.
  • Support for Melodie: Provide direct training and mentorship from Alex and Antwon to help her learn quickly.
